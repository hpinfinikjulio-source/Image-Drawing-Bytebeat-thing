Bytebeat ⇄ Image

Bytebeat

Image → Bytebeat (audio)

Audio is produced by scanning image pixels left-to-right, top-to-bottom and mapping grayscale to amplitude.

Audio → Bytebeat Image

Audio is converted to an 8-bit bytebeat-like buffer, then scanned into pixels and audio using the same mapping as images.

Video → Bytebeat (audio)

The video is sampled at the chosen frames-per-second; each sampled frame becomes one 8‑bit sound frame.

Simple, clean, mobile-friendly. Noto Sans.
